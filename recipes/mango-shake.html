Mango Shake

Description

Mango is everyone's favorite in summers and what's better than a chilled Mango Shake on a hot day

Ingredients

  • Mango (Duh!)
  • Milk (200ml for 1 glass)

Steps

  1. Peel the mango and cut it in small peices
  2. Add milk and mango pieces in mixer/blender
  3. Blend for 2-3 mins
  4. Serve chilled
