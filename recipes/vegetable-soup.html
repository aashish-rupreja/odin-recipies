Vegetable Soup

Description

Vegetable is soup is a great option for breakfast and is recommended if you have cold or fever.

Ingredients

  • Your choice of veggies (tomato, onion and peas are most commonly used)
  • Water
  • Spices (as per preference)

Steps

  1. Finely chop the veggies
  2. Add chopped veggies and water in a stainless steel pot
  3. Add some black pepper powder and chilli powder (per your preference)
  4. Wait till water starts boiling and you're done
