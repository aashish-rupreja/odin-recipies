Maggie

Description

Maggie is a quick to make lightweight breakfast which is favorited by many youngsters. It is available at a very nominal price and can be made without much hassle

Ingredients

  • 1 Pack of Maggie Noodles
  • Water
  • Vegetables(as per your preference)

Steps

  1. Add water and maggie in a stainless steel pot
  2. Add chopped vegetables(as per your preference)
  3. Add maggie masala included in the pack
  4. Let it cook for 2 minutes

Thats it! Your maggie is ready!
