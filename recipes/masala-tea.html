Masala Tea

Description

Everyone's favourite morning/evening drink which has many health benefits as well. Best enjoyed in monsoon and in winter season.

Ingredients

  • Tea powder(Chai patti)
  • Sugar(quantity as per preference)
  • Ginger
  • Cloves
  • Elaichi

Steps

  1. Add 2 table spoons of chai patti
  2. Add chai masala(ginger, cloves and elaichi)
  3. Add water and put it on light flame till water begins to boil
  4. Add milk
  5. Wait 10-15 minutes and your Masala Chai is ready

    6. You can add sugar before or after chai is made, its completely your preference
