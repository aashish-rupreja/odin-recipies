Veggie Sandwich

Description

Healthy snack for brekfast. Veg sandwich is go to choice of many youngsters because its easy to make and its healthy and tasty as well

Ingridents

  • 2 Bread Slices
  • Vegetables(Tomato, Onion, Cucumber, Beat are commonly used, feel free to add or remove as per your preference)
  • Sandwich Maker(optional)

Steps

  1. Cut vegetables into thin slices
  2. Put the vegetables between bread slices and you're veggie sandwich is ready!

OR

  1. Put your sandwich in the sandwich maker if you prefer toast sandwich
